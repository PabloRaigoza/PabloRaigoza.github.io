About Me

I am Pablo Raigoza, a Computer Science student at Cornell University graduating in 2025. I grew up in northern California. I am currently interested in pursuing a PhD in Computer Science. Prior to Cornell University I graduated from Chico Senior Highschool in 2022. Since I have began working with Dr. DingXin Cheng, a Civil Engineering professor at California State University, Chico. We have been researching pothole detection, with the purpose of area/volume estimation and a computer vision challenge to produce high-quality road scans using inexpensive equipment such as a GoPro.
I have interned at Naval Air Warfare Center Aircraft Division (NAWCAD) in Patuxent River, MD as an Navel Research Enterprise Internship Program (NREIP) intern. The lab I worked in was Quantum Research & Applications Branch (QRAB) where my work primarily focuses on real-time data acquisition and parallel processing for quantum correlations.
